This visualisations can be used the following way: bellow you can select the lift, gender of the lifters and the equipment that is used. When submitted, these qualities will be shown in the scatterplot. When you click on a dot in the scatterplot, the barchart will show the personal results of that powerlifter. The radarchart bellow the scatterplot will correspond to the gender and the lift that you submitted, and will show results of all equipment with these qualities accordingly. When you click on a box in the boxplot, the spiderchart on the buttom of the page will be updated. The spiderchart shows the results of lifters for each age group, with the equipment that you selected (clicked on) in the boxplot graph.
